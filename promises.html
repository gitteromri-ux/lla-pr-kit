Promise Contractually guaranteed, permanent live page on that exact site, with a link you can send. Refund if it fails. Say it out loud.
Likely Reached via the vendor's guaranteed feed (Benzinga / EIN network). Appears most of the time, but is not individually guaranteed and links may rotate off. Say "expected," not "guaranteed."
List name The logo only appears in the vendor's marketing "reach." It is a wire feed / affiliate reprint, not a guaranteed flagship article. Do NOT promise this.
