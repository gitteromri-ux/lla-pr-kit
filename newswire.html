Recommendation for the CEO

Fastest + cheapest: EIN Presswire ($99–149) — live in hours, AP News + Google News.
Widest reach / best "as-seen-on" value: BrandPush Popular ($265) — 200–300 outlets, but 2–7 days.
Genuine AP + financial prestige: Accesswire National ($775), unlimited length.
