Paid Newswire Options — Board Decision Sheet

Longevity Life Academy PR blitz · Verified 14 July 2026 · One release, one-time fee

◀ Submission Kit (free outlets) Paid Newswire Options
⏱ TIME-SENSITIVE: Go-live speed varies a lot. EIN Presswire = same day / within hours (fastest). BrandPush = 2–7 business days (no same-day, no weekend publishing). If the launch must be live this week, that difference is the deciding factor.

✅ Recommendation for the board

Best value + reach: BrandPush Popular ($265) for the widest "as-seen-on" footprint.
If speed is critical: EIN Presswire ($99–149) — the only same-day option here, live within hours + Google News in ~15 min.
If AP/Tier-1 prestige is required: Accesswire National ($775).
